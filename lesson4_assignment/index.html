No More Paper Delivery

Newspapers use to be the best way to get the news, you would open up the paper and catch up on what's going on in your neck of the woods.There would be coupons and advertisements for all sorts of differet companies. According to Statisa, online advertisements generate around 83 billion dollars yearly while newspapers only bring in 13 billion. These stats prove that online advertising brings in much more revenue than actual physical copies. With the advent of the internet, newspapers are becoming more absolute yearly. Many believe that newspapers are a waste of environmental resources, because we have such easy access to news at the tips of fingers.
